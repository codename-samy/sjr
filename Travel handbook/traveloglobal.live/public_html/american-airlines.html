American Airlines

We are sure you’re more than familiar with American Airlines, considering it’s the world’s largest Airlines. It is headquartered in Dallas/Fort Worth. American Airlines is a founding member of the Oneworld alliance. The airline has a strong brand image for onboard entertainment content. British Airways go by the formula of 4P’s. It is what drives them to top the charts for providing a fabulous flight experience. The classic 4P’s are: Product, Price, Promotion and Place. You would be surprised to know that American Airlines offer an average of nearly 6,700 flights per day in more than 50 countries.

Here’s why you must experience a flight with American Airlines-

Wide range of destinations: With flights more than to 350 destinations around the globe. The airline offers nonstop flights to over 130 international destinations in more than 55 countries, which includes London, Tokyo, Sydney and Rio De Janeiro as well. If we talk about domestic flights, the airline has an extensive domestic network with nonstop flights to over 200 destinations within the United States. Major routes and hubs: The airline operates from 10 primary hubs across the United States. These include Dallas/Fort worth International Airport, Miami International airport, Los Angeles International airport, Charlotte Douglas International airport, New York’s John F.Kennedy International airport, Chicago O’Hare International airport, La Guardia Airport, Phoenix Sky Harbor International airport and Philadelphia International airport. Fleet: The airline has a large fleet of 937 which is the largest commercial fleet in the world consisting of Airbus and Boeing. The airline has narrow body aircraft mainly Airbus 320 series and Boeing 737-800. American Airlines is also the largest operator of Airbus A319 and A321 variants along with A737 and A737-800 variants. Customer satisfaction: American Airlines consistently receives good reviews from customers for their friendly staff, comfortable seats, and prompt service. The on board and off-board staff provides quality service and caters to each and every need of the passengers making sure they have a lovely flight experience. Discount and deals: The Airline frequently offers discount, promotional offers, and rewards programs for loyal customers such as: AAdvantage-It is airline’s frequent flyer program. Passengers can redeem tickets, take advantage of upgrading service class, and have the option to go for car rentals, hotel stays and merchandise etc at discounted prices or absolutely free. Admirals Club- It was organized to appreciate the loyalty of its passengers who have been with American Airline, including celebrities, politician, other VIP’s and other ordinary customers as well. Flagship Lounge-Lounge specifically designed for passengers flying in first class and business class on international and transcontinental domestic flights. Latest technology: American Airlines is continually investing in the latest technology to improve their service, which includes mobile apps, in-flight Wi-Fi, and self-service kiosks. Flexible Fare: Flexible fare policy allows you to change your flight without collecting a service fee. Amazing, right? Safety records: American Airlines has a strong safety record and prioritizes safety above all else. Eco-friendliness: The Airline is committed to reducing its environmental impact and has taken steps to improve fuel efficiency and reduce waste. Joint ventures- American Airlines has joint ventures with the following airlines: British Airways, Finnair, Iberia, Japan Airlines and Qantas. American Airlines operates the largest commercial fleet (933 aircraft) in the world including both Airbus and Boeing. Services: When we talk about the comfort, the airline leaves no stone unturned for its passengers to have a wonderful flight experience. They have several cabin class services to offer: Flagship First, Flagship Business consisting of fully lie-flat seats; Domestic First class including complimentary alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages on all flights; Premium Economy offered on all wide body aircraft, wider in seats and passengers can enjoy two free checked bags, priority boarding and enhanced food and drink service including free alcohol. This product made American Airlines the first U.S carrier to offer a four-cabin aircraft, adding another feather in the cap! The other two class services offered are Main Cabin Extra which is its enhanced economy product and then we have Main Cabin, found on all mainline and regional aircrafts in its fleet. Trying it to make more affordable for its passengers, American airline also offers Basic Economy service, the lowest main cabin fare, on many routes.

Achievements: Over the years, American Airlines have achieved several milestones and accolades. In 1934, the airline became the first one in the industry to offer transcontinental services between New York and Los Angeles. In 1959, the airline commenced a computerized reservation system and was the first airline to do so. In 2013, American Airlines merged with the US Airways to become the world’s largest airlines. In 2015, it became the first airline to run commercial flights between the United States and Cuba in more than 50 years. In 2020, a cleanliness program called clean commitment was set in motion to ensure the safety and well being of passengers and employees amid COVID-19 pandemic. Overall, American Airline is undoubtedly one of the best globally, be it its vast network of destinations, modern aircraft or its customer service, staff, in-flight services offered and commitment to provide an optimum service to its passengers.
