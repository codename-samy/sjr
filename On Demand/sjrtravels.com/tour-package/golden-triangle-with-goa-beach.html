Daywise Itinerary

Day 1 : Delhi arrival and transfer

Delhi, the rich historical and the capital city of the Federal Republic of India. On arrival at Delhi international airport you will be received by our professional representative, who will be waiting for an Indian traditional welcome just outside of the airport (customs area) by your nameplate/ your referral. Proceed for hotel and check in and relax. Evening free to enjoy the city of joy. Over night stay at Delhi.
 

Day 2 : Delhi – Jaipur
Welcome to Jaipur, the pink city and Rajasthan’s colourful capital with its impressive monuments, an excellent range of handicrafts, a wide variety of cuisine and entertainment. Check in at hotel. Over night stay in jaipur hotel
Breakfast
Day 3 : Jaipur sightseeing
Today after breakfast drive for sightseeing tour of Jaipur and start with magnificent Amer Palace, a fascinating blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture, this red sandstone and white marble palace has some very interesting apartment, the likes of which are not to be found anywhere in the country. Visit City Palace, with its fabulous museum that displays some of the most amazing treasures held by the jaipur royal family. Also visit Jantar Mantar, the unique observatory museum built by the founder of Jaipur-Sawai Jai Singh. Also visit many more historical and magnificent monuments. Back to hotel and over night stay.
Breakfast
Day 4 : Jaipur – Agra
Today after breakfast embark for Agra .Welcome to Agra - in the northern Indian state of Uttar Pradesh and the city of Taj Mahal-One of the seven wonder of the world. Visit Taj Mahal- stands on the banks of the Yamuna river. It was built in the memory of the beautiful Arjumand Bano Begum, who won the heart of a Mughal prince. Work on the mausoleum began in 1633 and 20,000 workers laboured for 17 years to build it. The most skilled architects, inlay craftsmen, calligraphers, stone-carvers and masons came from all across India and lands as distant as Persia and Turkey. Taj Mahal has inspired poets, painters and musicians to try and capture its elusive magic in word, colour and son. Since the 17th century, travellers have crossed continents to come and see this ultimate memorial to love, and few have been unmoved by its incomparable beauty.
Breakfast
Day 5 : Agra – Delhi
Today after half day agra sightseeing tour you will drive back to Delhi. On arrival at Delhi check in at your hotel. Evening free for delighted enjoyment in the night city of Delhi. Over night stay in hotel.
Breakfast
Day 6 : Delhi sightseeing
Today after breakfast start your guided amazing sightseeing tour of Delhi. Visit India Gate, which is 160 feet high with an arch of 138 feet. Built as a memorial to commemorate the 70,000 India soldiers killed in World War I, India Gate was designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens and completed in 1931. Visit Red Fort- the mughal emperor, Shah Jahan, after ruling from Agra for elleven years, decided to shift to Delhi and laid the foundation stone of the Red Fort in 1618. It is called so because of the red stone with which it is built, the Red Fort is one of the most magnificent palaces in the world. Visit Qutub Minar, Jama Masjit, Teen Murti House-thismemorial was built in memory of the Indian soldiers who perished in World War I - The Teen Murti Bhavan housed the first Prime Minister of India, Jawaharlal Nehru. Also visit shanty Van, Rastrapati Bhawan-was completed in 1929 at a cost of 12,53,000 pound sterling, and finally Lotus Temple.
Breakfast
Day 7 : Delhi – Fly to Goa
Today drive to Delhi airport to catch flight for Goa. On arrival Goa airport transfer to Hotel. Evening free.
Breakfast
Day 8 : North Goa sightseeing tour
View of Fort Aguada-The fort was one of the most important places in Goa and the most crucial of all forts in Goa. Today it is being used as a jail. The Church of Mae de Deus at Saligao, Temple of Lord Bodgeshwa,Map usa Town, Vagator Beach, Anjuna Beach, Calangute Beach.
Breakfast
Day 9 : South Goa sightseeing tour
Miramar Beach, Casket containing sacred relics of Saint Francis Xavier, Se Cathedral- This is thelargest cathedral in Asia. The Se cathedral has five bells out of which one is the famous Golden Bell which is one the largest in the world. Shantadurga Temple , Colva Beach-Famous as the longest beach in Goa, Colva beach is the most popular beach in south Goa. The beach provides plenty of opportunities for shell collectors.
Breakfast
Day 10 : Leisure in Goa Beach.
This is the Leisure day for relaxe in incredible Goa sea beach, popularly known as Golden Beach of India. Most comfortable weather of Goa attracs millions of tourist from the world.
Breakfast
Day 11 : Fly for onward destination.
Today after breakfast Transfer to Goa Airport to catch flight for Mumbai / Delhi International Airport for ownward destination.
Breakfast
