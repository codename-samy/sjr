Icelandair Airlines

Icelandair is Iceland's principal airline and flag carrier, with headquarters at Reykjavik Airport (RKV) in Reykjavik. Air Iceland and Eagle Air are two more airlines that operate out of the airport. Icelandair codeshares with Aeroflot, Air Iceland Connect, Alaska Airlines, Cabo Verde Airlines, Finnair, JetBlue, Scandinavian Airlines, and Sun Country Airlines.

Iceland's geographic location enables for one-stop transatlantic flights, which is part of the airline's business plan. Since 1950, the airline has been a member of IATA, the Association of European Airlines since 1957, and the Flight Safety Foundation since 1966.

TRAVEL CLASS

1.Economy Class: Economy Class is ideal for travellers looking for value and comfort.

Passengers in Economy Class may take use of a variety of amenities, including complementary non-alcoholic refreshments and access to in-flight entertainment systems. If you want to buy alcoholic beverages, food, or WI-FI, you may do so onboard for a price.

Passengers are permitted to check in a luggage weighing up to 23kg.

2.Business Class: From the airport through the landing, the Business Class Saga Premium service provides customers with a luxurious flying experience.

The Saga Business Class lounge is created in the unique Nordic design style. Passengers may have a tasty lunch while admiring the breathtaking panoramic vista. Business Class guests will find everything they need before their trip, whether it's a shower to freshen up or free Wi-Fi to catch up on work. The SAGA lounge caters to both business and leisure travellers, including amenities for children and families. Passengers on Saga may also bypass lines by heading straight to priority boarding and check-in.

3.Saga Premium Flex : is an upgrade on Business Class that provides premium comfort and exclusive amenities as well as greater flexibility (including easier ticket changes and full refundability).

LUGGAGE INFORMATION

1. SAGA CLASS: Passengers in the Saga Class are permitted two carry-on bags per passenger, in addition to one small personal item. Saga Class passengers are also permitted two checked baggage weighing up to 32kg and measuring up to 158cm in length.

Each carry-on bag is limited to a maximum weight of 10kg and dimensions of 40cm x 30cm x 15cm. The airline permits two carry-on bags per person, plus one small personal item, for Economy comfort.

2. ECONOMY CLASS: Passengers are permitted one carry-on bag per person and one small personal item.

Passengers are permitted to bring one piece of baggage weighing up to 23kg. These items are intended for flights inside Europe.

Passengers in Economy Comfort are permitted two checked baggage weighing up to 23kg apiece.

CHECK-IN INFORMATION:

Check-In Online: Passengers may check in online by going to the Alternative Airlines website and clicking on the airline's logo. To finish the check-in process, input your flight information. Web check-in begins 36 hours before departure for international flights and 24 hours before departure for domestic flights.

Check-In at the Airport: Passengers can check-in for their flight at the airport by visiting the check-in counter or using one of the self-service kiosks.
