About AirAsia

AirAsia is a low-cost airline headquartered at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Being the largest airline in Malaysia, it operates scheduled domestic and international flights to more than 165 destinations across 25 countries. Its main hub airport is Klia 2 and registered office in Petaling Jaya Selangor.

Frequently Asked Questions about AirAsia

Q. Which cities are majorly covered by AirAsia?

There are 8 AirAsia flights every week to Honolulu, US. Other major destinations that are covered by AirAsia include Auckland, Bangkok, Beijing, Colombo, Cebu, Mauritius, Paris. It is known for its services in the major parts in India, China, the U.K., and the U.S.

Q. What are the baggage rules of AirAsia for checked and hand baggage?

Passengers can carry two cabin baggage on board either. Cabin baggage not more than 56cm (H) ×36cm (W)×23cm(D). A laptop bag or small handbag not more than 40cm (H)× 30cm (w)×23cm(D). As for the checked baggage, you are allowed to carry 1 bag in their Flexible Class and 2 bags in the Business Class. Each of the bags must not weigh more than 50 lbs and must have a maximum of 62 inches of linear dimensions.

Q. What is the check-in process of AirAsia?

You can check-in on your AirAsia flight by visiting their online website by mentioning your booking number and name or surname. Check-in opening time- 7 days to 4 hours before the scheduled time to departure.
