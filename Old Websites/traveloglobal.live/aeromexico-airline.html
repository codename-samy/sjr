Aeromexico Airlines

Aeroméxico is a Mexican air carrier in Mexico City International Airport, Monterrey International Airport. Aeromexico and its affiliate, Aeromexico Link, are members of Group Aeromexico, S.A.B de C.V, a holding company whose branches offer commercial aircraft services in Mexico.

The airline is a founder partner of the SkyTeam Alliance and has expanded its relationship with Delta Air Lines-which owns the majority of Aeroméxico through a joint promotional arrangement, shares details, costs and profits on all flights between both the United States and Mexico.

Seating Class

Class in Economy:

Offers a great menu on all planes, a wide variety of movies for free, TV shows and music to help time travel on board your flight and stay connected to free texting and wifi.

Class in Premium Economy:

Enjoy the advantages of the premium Economy Class (AM Plus) providing additional legroom, overhead storage bins, priority boarding, check-in and luggage, and exposure to the Salon Premier and Heinken Terrace (for a special rate.)

Business Class:

Class Premier Class is the designation of Aeromexico's luxury offering, offered on the 787 Dreamliner. Take it easy in the private Premier Lounge, or one of the mutual international lounges of the carrier, before returning to the sky. Full-bed seats are fitted with flexible headrests, electronically flexible lumbar support and height-adjustable footrests. Cherish the latest entertainment on sophisticated seat-back entertainment systems with an exclusive on-board menu that has been carefully planned by Elena Reygadas, a world-renowned Mexican chef.

Luggage Allowance

The luggage allocation depends on the fare selected and the start and destination of the voyage. You can now book your luggage on your flight at the time of booking, check our additional baggage page and find out how to book extra baggage.

Carry on Luggage

The simple and main cabin fares comprise one regular piece of carry-on luggage plus one personal item that can be transported to the cabin. Standard luggage item and small backpack both need to have a size limit of 55cm x 40cm x 25cm (21.5 in x 15.7 in x 10 in). Combined, the two things must weigh up to a limit of 10 kg (22 lb).

The overall combined weight of carry-on baggage for Class Premier Passengers is 18kg (39 lb). - Object shall measure up to or less than 55cm x 40cm x 25cm (21.5 in x 15.7 in x 10 in).

Checked Luggage

Please notice that all checked bags must reach up to a maximum linear size of 62 inches (158 cm). Excess luggage charges will apply for overweight luggage.

Check-In Information

Online Check-in:

Online check-in is valid for international flights 24 hours prior to boarding. Check-in is valid for flights inside Mexico up to 48 hours before departure. Check-in is accessible on the Aeromexico check-in website.

Check-in at the airport:

For worldwide planes and routes, commuters could check-in at the airport 24-48 hours prior departure and up to 2 hours prior to departure for planes within Mexico. Self-service check-in kiosks at terminals are accessible at a variety of international airports to accelerate the check-in procedure at the airport.

Frequent Flyer Programme

Club Premier is a free loyalty reward service. Commuters should sign up on the airline's website to start winning points while flying with Aeromexico or the Skyteam partnership airline. When purchasing your Aeromexico flights with us, it is easy to pick 'Club Premier' from the drop-down category mostly during booking process, then easily add your specific membership number and earn your points.
