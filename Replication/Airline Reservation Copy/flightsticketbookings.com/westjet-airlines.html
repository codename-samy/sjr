WestJet Airlines

Established in 1996, it is the second largest airline in Canada. With 184 aircraft in its fleet, this iconic air carrier serves approximately 160 cities around the world. If you're an avid globetrotter, a seasonal traveler or someone who flies regularly for work, you won't have any difficulty making WestJet reservations for your popular destinations. Travel Class

Economy Class Economy class varies according to the type of aircraft flying with WestJet. On the WestJet 787, economic seats have a pitch of 31" and are offered in a 3-3-3 setup. Complementary food and drinks (non-alcoholic drinks) are included when traveling overseas, while, on WestJet's 767, economy seats have a pitch of 31" and are provided in a 2-3-2 configuration.

Premium Economy: Premium is housed in a different, reserved cabin. Larger seats with larger recline are available in configuration 2-3-2 in 787, configuration 2-2-2 in 767, specification 2-2 in 737Max and setup 3-3 in 737-600,-700 and-800 WestJet.

Business Class: On 787 WestJet Airlines planes, Business Class featuring private pods with direct aisle access is provided. Business Class aircraft in WestJet carry mechanical flat beds in a 1-2-1 setup.

BAGGAGE POLICY:

Travelers with WestJet Flight Tickets are permitted to carry 1 piece of luggage and 1 personal item as a carrier. The measurements of the carrying bag shall not exceed 21 x 15 x 9 inches, while the measurements of the personal piece shall not exceed 16 x 13 x 6 inches.

Customers travelling in Business Class and Premium Class can take 2 pieces of checked luggage free of charge. People flying with EconoFlex Fare can take 1 piece of luggage free of charge. Passengers flying with Basic and Econo Fare do not have the option of carrying any checked luggage free of charge. All levels of travel shall be permitted to carry up to a limit of four bags. The weight of each bag cannot be more than 50 lb, whilst the linear size cannot be more than 62 inches.

CHECK-IN INFORMATION:

You will check-in WestJet Airlines flights online by visiting the carrier's official website. You should check-in online and print your boarding pass at home to save time. Online check-in is valid from 24 hours to 1 hour for transatlantic passengers and up to 45 minutes before scheduled departure time for domestic flights.
