North East India Tour Packages

The Northeast is the culmination of breathtaking beauty, charming landscapes, magical waterfalls, wildlife, natural wonders and some amazing hospitable people that welcome you with their open hearts. North East India comprises of seven states namely Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura. Northeast is the unexplored beauty that needs more recognition as it is beautiful beyond limits but isn't as popular as it should be. The Seven Sisters is the name given to the Northeast as it has seven beautiful states that culminate into a beautiful Northeast. There is something in store for everyone here. You will find the glacial lakes, Pilgrimages, Wildlife, Waterfalls, and some amazing Lakes that are too beautiful to be true. Nature is best conserved and the development here is sustainable and not so stressed on bringing development or foreign investments. Give yourself the best of untouched Northeast and the best of the Seven Sisters that our packages provide to you.

The Culture

The Northeast has the most interesting culture that is completely diverse and impossible to believe. Their traditions are years old and there exist a large number of tribes under the geographical boundaries of the Northeast. You can witness here various traditions, festivals, dance, customs and heritage that is different for everyone. The main festivals that the Northeast can provide to you are the Bihu, Ke Pemblang, Nongkrem, Durga Puja and various other cultural festivals that are held throughout the year. Some of the dance forms are also very popular. Some of them are the Garia, Bihu, Hai Hak, and Manipuri. Witness the best of the Northeast and see what they have for you to explore.  

Things to do :

  • Trek along the beautiful nature trails of the Northeast states
  • See some of the most beautiful glacial lakes here
  • Explore the amazing Singalila Ridge
  • Try Helicopter rides and take your first flight
  • Home to various exquisite Monasteries that you can visit
