Welcome to Our Cookie Policy

Discover how we use cookies to enhance your experience on our Websites and Apps. All capitalized terms not defined herein can be found in our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a web server saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. It allows the website to remember your actions and preferences, such as login details, language, and font size, for future visits. Cookies can also be used for analytics and marketing purposes, such as tracking site visits and retargeting visitors with advertisements.

Types of Cookies and Their Uses:

On Airline Fare Center, we may utilize the following types of cookies:

1st Party Cookies:
First-party cookies are set by Airline Fare Center for users on its website.

3rd Party Cookies:
Third-party cookies are set by Airline Fare Center's trusted partners.

Session Cookies:
Session cookies are temporary and remain on your device until you close your web browser. Many session cookies are essential for the proper functioning of Airline Fare Center, enabling you to browse, search, and book smoothly.

Persistent Cookies:
Persistent cookies stay on your device longer or until you manually delete them. They help recognize you as an existing user, making your interactions with the website more seamless. For instance, you won't have to log in every time you visit.

Strictly Necessary Cookies:
These cookies are essential for Airline Fare Center's proper functioning, including login functionality, load balancing, and form filling.

Performance Cookies:
Performance cookies track metrics like visit count and page popularity, optimizing your user experience.

Functional Cookies:
Functional cookies enhance the website's features based on your preferences, like language choice and booking settings.

Advertising Cookies:
Advertising cookies ensure relevant ads on Airline Fare Center and related sites, helping analyze marketing campaign performance and ad frequency.

Analytics and Personalization Cookies:
These cookies analyze your usage to offer personalized travel recommendations, offers, and deals.

Social Media Cookies:
Social media cookies enable interactions with content on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.

Managing and Controlling Your Cookies
While we use 1st-party and 3rd-party cookies to enhance your experience, you have control. Some third-party providers offer opt-out options for behavioral or targeted ads. You can also adjust your browser settings and delete cookies manually. Note that this may affect certain website features requiring cookie data.

Note: By using our website and services, you accept this Cookies Policy. For more on privacy settings and personal data usage, visit our Privacy Policy page.
