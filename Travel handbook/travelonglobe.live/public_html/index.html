From coast to coast Australia is a country which offers it all.

See yourself snorkelling the famous Great Barrier Reef one day, then troop around the worlds largest sand island by 4x4 the next. Try your luck at rustic camping under the stars around the iconic Ayers Rock, or maybe bask on the white sands of the Whitsunday Islands.

You choose your own adventure, and we are here to help put it all together. With budgeted East Coast Packages and overland tours available through the outback, getting around has never been easier. 

We are travel company and offers independent tours and small private group tours. The company was created by experienced travelers, who have visited more than 90 countries as a desire to share their passion for traveling and for exploring what our planet has to offer.

We offer itineraries in Europe, Asia, North and South America, Africa and Australia. The trips include design hotels accommodation, transport, a great number of activities and entertainment and are focused to immerse travelers into the atmosphere of the visited countries and cities. The travelers will explore the chosen route without nerving tour leaders and large touristic groups, but just meet local guides when visiting certain attractions. The motivation behind this was to give an opportunity for travelers not just to go on perfectly organized adventures but also to escape from touristic crowds, learn the local language, live like a local and privately explore the countries.

We understand that each traveler enjoys different styles and some prefer breathtaking beaches with white sand, surfing and huge waves, another one prefer getting lost in a huge city, living on the rooftop and having a morning run in Central Park. We keep that in mind and create adventures that have a great range of activities and will suit you perfectly. The only thing that stays the same is the level of comfort. We are working with top leading design hotels and provide our travelers with comfortable private transportation
