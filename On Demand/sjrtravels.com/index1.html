Incredible North East India

Tour Highlights

North East India 8 Days tours package itinerary will take you to worlds most wildlife preserve areas of North east Indian tourist destinations. Guwahati, Shillong, Cherapunjee, Kazirang National Park. Kaziranga National Park was announce as World Heritage Site in 1985 by UNESCO. Kaziranga is very rich for its varied and Rare kind of wildlife population as well as the unique cultures of Local Tribal people. Along with Kaziranga national park we include Shillong and Cherapunjee to experience e the heaviest rainfall areas in the world in this tour.  Your tour will start from Guwahati It is the gateway to the North eastern states of India and a principal centre of socio-cultural, political, industrial and trade and commerce of the entire region. You have a visit Holi Kamakhya temple as well as a soundless sailing on the Holi Brahmaputra River.
