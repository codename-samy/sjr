Frequently Asked Questions about Flights to Detroit

Q. When should I fly to Detroit?

If you plan to visit Detroit, then it's highly recommended by FareObuddy to have your travels to the city from June till August. The weather in Detroit is hot-summer humid, which the Great Lakes positively influence. The winters are cold, with a moderate amount of snowfall. For enjoying Detroit around the less busy timeline, you can also plan your trip around April to May and September to October.

Q. Which day is the cheapest to fly to Detroit?

Travel experts at FareObuddy observed that booking tickets to Detroit are cheaper on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

Q. What time of day is the cheapest to buy a flight ticket to Detroit?

FareObuddy travel experts suggest that the best time to buy an online ticket to Detroit is 3 pm EST.

Q. What are the options of the cheapest Airlines that fly to Detroit?

The cheapest airline option to fly to Detroit is Frontier Airlines.

Q. How long before should I be at the airport for my flight to Detroit?

You must reach the baggage desk for your flight to Detroit at least 3 hours before the flight departure.

Q. What is the name of Detroit's main airport and subsidiary airports?

The major international airport to fly to Detroit is Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport.

Q. How long is the flight to Detroit from New York?

Direct flights from New York to Detroit that are available on FareObuddy take approximately 1 hour 58 minutes.

Q. What are the top attractions in Detroit that I must visit?

The top attractions to visit during your stay in Detroit are:

  • Charles H Wright Museum of African American History
  • Belle Isle
  • Henry Ford Museum
  • GM Renaissance Center
  • Masonic Temple of Detroit

Q. Which airlines fly to Detroit from New York?
