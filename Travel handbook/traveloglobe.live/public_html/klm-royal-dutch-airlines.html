KLM Airlines

KLM Royal Dutch Airlines is one of the biggest airlines in the Netherlands which was established on 7 October 1919 which also makes it the oldest airline carrier which is still operating with its original name. KLM Airlines is headquartered in Amstelveen and its hub located in Amsterdam Airport Schiphol.

KLM Royal Dutch Airlines operates with 4 classes as:

  • ● Travel classes
  • ● World Business Class
  • ● Europe Business Class
  • ● Economy Class.

Baggage Policy

The baggage allowance can differ from different flights. The pieces of information regarding the baggage will be shown to you while you book your flight through our web portal or KLM Airlines Application.

Hand baggage allowance

Economy Class on European and intercontinental flights

  • ● 1 item of hand baggage, max. 55 x 35 x 25 cm (21,5 x 13,5 x 10 inch)
  • ● 1 accessory, e.g. a handbag, briefcase or laptop, max. 40 x 30 x 15 cm (16 x 12 x 6 inch)
  • ● Total weight max. 12 kg (26 lbs).

Business Class on European and intercontinental flights

  • ● 2 items of hand baggage, each max. 55 x 35 x 25 cm (21,5 x 13,5 x 10 inch)
  • ● 1 accessory, e.g. a handbag, briefcase or laptop, max. 40 x 30 x 15 cm (16 x 12 x 6 inch)
  • ● Total weight max. 18 kg (40 lbs)

Check-in baggage allowance

Economy Class on European and intercontinental flights

  • ● 1 item of check-in baggage*: L + W + H max. 158 cm (62 inch) and max. 23 kg (50 lbs).
  • ● Flying Blue Silver, Gold, and Platinum members and SkyTeam Elite and Elite Plus members can bring 1 extra item of check-in baggage.

Business Class on European and intercontinental flights

  • ● 2 items of check-in baggage: each L + W + H max. 158 cm (62 inch) and each max. 32 kg (70,5 lbs)
  • ● Flying Blue Silver, Gold, and Platinum members and SkyTeam Elite and Elite Plus members can bring 1 extra item of check-in baggage.

Changing or canceling flights

A customer can change their itinerary even if they bought flexibility ticket type or not. The change and cancellation of your flight depending on the conditions applied to your ticket which will be mentioned in the email you received after booking your ticket. You need to make sure that your flight ticket number should start with ‘074’ (KLM), ‘057’ (Air France), or with '006' (Delta Air Lines).

To change your ticket online

  • ● Log in to your KLM Airlines account through a web application or mobile application, go to your flight details and click the button ‘Change flights’
  • ● Now you can select the itinerary change or cancellation option.
  • ● Just proceed with it and there are any changes regarding changing your itinerary you will responsible to pay that amount to make a change in your itinerary.

To change your ticket offline

You can directly call us at 0208-0664998. to the helpline number which is 24x7 available.

Check-In Information

Check-In Online

You can directly check -In online through the App or website and drop your luggage at the airport while you board your flight.

Online Check-In options are available 30 hours before departure (24 hours before in case of flights to or from the United States).

Check-in at the Airport

We also provide the Check-In option at the airport. You can walk towards the Check-In desk and the KLM executives will be happy to assist you with the Check-In.

Check-in desks open only 2 hours prior to the boarding of the Flight.

You have all the rights to book your ticket without any hassle and to do so you can directly visit the KLM Airlines web portal and book your reservation, If you are not familiar with the technology then you can also call us at 0208-0664998.
