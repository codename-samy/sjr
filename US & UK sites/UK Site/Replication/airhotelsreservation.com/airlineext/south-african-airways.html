About South African Airways

South Africa's national flag carrier, South African Airways is based out of Johannesburg. The Airways flies to over 35 destinations all across the globe including Middle East, Africa, Australia, Europe, Asia and North & South America. Tracing back to their first flight in the year 1934, SAA did not look back and has welcomed the world to South Africa and successfully has managed to showcase the beautiful, gentle heart of the country.
South African Airways is a Star Alliance member. The airline has become a global airline company and its excellence has been rewarded with 14 Skytrax awards rewarding it to be the best African airline and a 4-star airline. This has led to building their dedication and innovation to another level. The current in-service fleet size of SAA has 47 aircraft which includes Airbus A320-200, Boeing 737-300F and Airbus A340-600. If you are a budget traveller, South African Airways has introduced a subsidiary airline company known as Mango. It is comparatively a low-cost domestic airline carrier.

South African Airways Cabins

South African Airways Economy Class

Live a memorable flying experience as you fly in South African Airways Economy Class which is known to offer comfort with generous leg space with its seating arrangement. Every flyer is given amenity kits that makes their experience more happening.
For your entertainment, a wide range of channels are offered that cater to their needs. If you are a movie buff, SAA offers a plethora of options for all the passengers, including kids and aged people. Alternatively, you can play games or listen to music as the in-flight entertainment system is perfect.

South African Airways Business Class

South African Airways Business Class is a treat for every traveller. The cabin crew offers everything to make your journey great.
They offer luxurious amenities and impressive services, starting from rejuvenating welcome drinks to fully-packed amenity kits. In case you are on a business trip, you will be provided with ample space to work and keep in contact with your colleagues while flying.
The seats of Business Class are made up of the finest fabric and leather seats of Business Class which can be reclined for a blissful sleep. To make it better, the staff offers plush pillows and soft cushions to add extra comfort.
Entertain yourself by watching your favourite movie or TV show on a personal screen and have a great overall experience.
Each seat comes with an inbuilt power socket and USB point to charge your devices.

Food Facilities Offered by South African Airways

The authentic South African cuisine and beverages which are offered on SAA are the highlight of the journey. The meals are freshly prepared by chefs Benny Masekwameng and Reuben Riffel with their signature dishes as well.
In case you are travelling Economy Class, the airline serves you with delectable snacks, sumptuous meals and the local South African products onboard.
You can savour tasty starters, a multi-course cuisine and desserts.
For both Economy and Business Class, the snacks are complimentary.
The menu of the airline includes an eye-pleasing and mouth-watering dishes prepared by the chefs.
In case you want to enjoy a vegetarian, there are several options in the Special Meal. All you have to do is place an order for it in 72 hours in advance.
