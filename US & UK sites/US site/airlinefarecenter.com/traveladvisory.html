Travel Guidelines

For all domestic travelers (those journeying within the United States), full vaccination is a requirement. Alternatively, you must undergo testing both before and after your trip.
For all travelers returning from an international destination to the United States, regardless of vaccination status, it is mandatory to present a negative COVID-19 test taken within 24 hours before arrival.

If you'd like to access comprehensive information about your travel destination, we recommend visiting the CDC Travel Advisory.
