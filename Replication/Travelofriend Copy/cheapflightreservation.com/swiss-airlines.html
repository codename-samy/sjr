Swiss International Airlines

The major carrier for Switzerland, is Swiss Air, offers several flights to destinations in Europe, North America, South America, Africa, and Asia. The airline is a member of the Star Alliance and has its primary hub at Zurich Airport. From Zurich airport, Swiss Airline ran regular flights under the name and corporate identity of its parent company, Swiss International. The airline was established on September 1st, 2005.

Swiss Airline Baggage Policy

The quantity of hand luggage bags is distributed by Swiss International Airlines in accordance with the kind of tickets purchased.

Swiss Economy is allowed one 8 kg bag.

2* 8 kg Bags are authorised for Swiss Business.

Swiss citizens are allowed two 8 kg bags.

Please be aware that all classes and bags must confirm that they meet the 55cm*40*23cm limit.

Checked Baggage

Light: No Baggage included.

Classic: 1* Max: 23 kg

Business -2* Max 32 kg

Baggage exceeding 32kg will not be accepted by the airline as there are strict rules.
