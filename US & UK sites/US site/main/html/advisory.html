Travel Advisory

COVID-19 Guidelines

As we navigate through the COVID-19 pandemic, the safety and well-being of our travelers remain our top priority. Please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Wear a mask or face covering in public places.
  • Maintain a safe distance of at least 6 feet from others.
  • Frequently wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol when soap and water are not available.
  • Stay home if you're feeling unwell or experiencing COVID-19 symptoms.
  • Follow local health and safety regulations at your destination.

General Travel Guidelines

Before embarking on your journey, please consider the following general travel guidelines:

  • Check travel restrictions and entry requirements for your destination.
  • Have necessary travel documents, including passports and visas.
  • Plan your itinerary and accommodations in advance.
  • Keep essential items like masks, hand sanitizer, and disinfectant wipes handy.
  • Stay informed about your airline's policies and any travel advisories.

Stay Safe and Informed

TravelQuads is committed to providing you with a safe and pleasant travel experience. However, circumstances may change, so it's essential to stay updated on travel advisories and guidelines from health authorities and governments.

Safe travels!
