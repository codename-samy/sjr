Privacy Policy

We have developed a privacy policy to keep you posted on what personally identifiable data ("PID") we may collect about you, what this data will be used for, with whom such data will be shared, and the security policies we have developed to protect your privacy. PID includes data that can be correlated to a specific individual, such as their name, address, phone number, or email address.

Personal Information: How We Collect It

To make travel arrangements and provide other services to you, we will collect personal data from you, including but not limited to:

  1. Your name, address, phone number, and email address
  2. Travel preferences such as seat and airline preferences
  3. Information necessary to facilitate travel or other services, including travel companion(s) names, emergency contacts, seating preferences, and medical needs
  4. Personal data provided to our representatives to research and resolve conflicts or answer questions
  5. Payment data, including but not limited to your credit card number, card type, cardholder name, expiration date, and billing address

We, at TravelQuads.live, understand your concern for privacy and take every step to protect the personal information of our customers, including their names, addresses, and contact details. For visiting the TravelQuads.live website, you don't need to reveal your personal information. Individual users exploring our website remain completely anonymous.

Cookies:

Some of our web pages use "Cookies" to serve our customers better and provide them with personalized information they need from our site. Cookies only identify the websites to facilitate your next visit to our site. Information collected through them is used only to analyze and improve our services for you. No individual information related to your identity is collected or used in this process.

PHISHING SCAMS

Please send us any scam/phishing emails you have received by reporting them here. If you have received an email that you believe to be a phishing scam please remember that it is very common for these scams to be reused at a later date with only slightly different message, such as a different subject or return address, or using the fake webpage(s) hosted on a different webserver. We submit and track all reports against phishing with IC3 at www.ic3.gov/complaint/default.aspx.

HOW WE UTILIZE Data WE COLLECT AND WITH WHOM IT IS SHARED

When you make travel reservations with TravelQuads.live and send information about yourself, we use this data to complete your order (i.e., to complete air, hotel, and other reservations), fulfill any customer service requests, and provide promotional or trip-related information via email. We do not share this information with third parties except to the extent necessary to complete your travel arrangements. Such parties may include, but are not limited to, appropriate airlines and other travel suppliers, credit card merchant services, airline reservation systems, etc. These service providers will disclose information about your traveler profile to hotel, airline, and other travel suppliers whose products you have purchased. Although TravelQuads.live travels ensures to select travel suppliers that are reputable companies, we have not placed restrictions on these suppliers from using or disclosing your information without your permission. Therefore, we encourage you to review the information privacy practices of any travel suppliers whose products you purchase on TravelQuads.live. These travel suppliers may also contact you as needed to obtain additional inputs about your confirmed reservation.

Data Safety of Your Personal Information

We recognize the importance of security for all PID attached to our customers and use reasonable steps consistent with industry practices to protect PID. However, no data transmission can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we will use top-of-the-line SSL to protect your personal information, we cannot warrant the security of any information you transmit to us. In order to safeguard the data stored in our systems, we have implemented security measures, including data encryption technology and firewalls.

Questions or Concerns

If you have questions or comments about this privacy policy, please send an email to us at info@travelquads.live. We welcome your comments or questions.

Prohibited Use Policy

As a precursor to your use of this Site, you assure that you will not use this website for any purpose that is unlawful or prohibited. You concur that you will not perform an action which would deliberately affect the site's performance and stability.
