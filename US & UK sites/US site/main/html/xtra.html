Price Match Policy

At TravelQuads, we are committed to providing you with the best possible travel deals. Our Price Match Policy ensures that you get the most competitive prices for your flights and travel bookings. Here's how it works:

How It Works

If you find a lower price for the same flight or travel package on another website within 24 hours of booking with us, we'll match that price or refund the difference. Our Price Match Policy is designed to give you peace of mind, knowing that you're getting the best value for your travel expenses.

Eligibility

To be eligible for our Price Match Policy, the following conditions must be met:

  • The lower-priced offer must be for the same itinerary, including flight details, dates, and times.
  • The offer must be publicly available and verifiable on the competitor's website at the time of our review.
  • Price Match requests must be submitted within 24 hours of booking your flight or travel package with us.

How to Request a Price Match

If you believe you have found a lower price that meets our eligibility criteria, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support team at [Customer Support Email or Phone Number] within 24 hours of booking.
  2. Provide our team with the following details:
    • Your booking reference number with us.
    • The URL of the competitor's website displaying the lower price.
    • Screenshots or any other evidence that clearly shows the lower price and relevant details.
  3. Our team will review the price match request and verify its eligibility.
  4. If the request is approved, we will match the price or refund the difference to your original payment method.

Terms and Conditions

Please note that our Price Match Policy is subject to the following terms and conditions:

  • We reserve the right to refuse a price match request if it does not meet our eligibility criteria or if we are unable to verify the lower price.
  • Price Match requests are limited to one per customer per booking.
  • Our Price Match Policy applies to the base fare of the flight or travel package and does not include taxes, fees, or additional services.
  • Price Match requests for bookings made through our affiliate or partner websites are not eligible.
  • We may modify or terminate our Price Match Policy at any time without prior notice.

At TravelQuads, we are dedicated to ensuring that you receive the best deals for your travel needs. If you have any questions or would like to request a price match, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team.

Taxes and Fees Policy

At TravelQuads, we are committed to providing transparent information about taxes and fees associated with booking travel services. Please review the following details to understand our policy:

Government Taxes

When booking airline tickets, hotels, or other travel services, government taxes may apply. These taxes are imposed by governmental authorities and can vary based on your travel destination, route, and class of service. The applicable taxes will be clearly displayed during the booking process, allowing you to review and accept them before finalizing your reservation.

Service Fees

TravelQuads may charge service fees for processing and facilitating your travel bookings. These fees are typically associated with the convenience and assistance we provide in securing your travel arrangements. The service fees, if applicable, will be disclosed before you confirm your booking.

Booking Fees

Some airlines and service providers may charge booking fees in addition to their base fares. These fees cover the cost of making reservations and issuing tickets. The specific booking fees, if any, will be outlined during the booking process, and you can choose to proceed with the booking or explore alternative options.

Baggage Fees

As mentioned in our Baggage Policy, baggage fees may apply for checked baggage and additional luggage. These fees are set by the airlines and may vary depending on the airline, travel class, and route. We recommend reviewing the baggage fees associated with your chosen airline before booking your flight.

Payment Processing Fees

When making payments for your bookings, credit card companies or payment processors may charge transaction fees or currency conversion fees. These fees are beyond our control and are determined by the respective financial institutions. Please check with your payment provider for details on any additional charges.

Transparent Pricing

TravelQuads is committed to providing transparent pricing. We strive to clearly communicate all applicable taxes and fees associated with your bookings upfront. You will have the opportunity to review and accept these charges before completing your reservation.

If you have any questions or require further clarification regarding taxes and fees, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team. We are here to assist you and ensure your travel booking experience is seamless and hassle-free.

Post Ticketing Fees Policy

At TravelQuads, we are committed to transparency and ensuring that our customers have a clear understanding of our pricing policies. We want to clarify our stance on post-ticketing fees:

No Hidden Fees

We take pride in offering straightforward pricing for our flight tickets. When you book a flight through TravelQuads, the price you see at the time of booking is the final price you'll pay. We do not charge any hidden or surprise fees after ticketing.

All-Inclusive Pricing

Our ticket prices are all-inclusive, covering the base fare, taxes, and any applicable fees. You won't encounter additional charges for standard services such as seat selection, cabin baggage, or online check-in.

Fee Transparency

If there are any fees associated with optional services or upgrades, such as priority boarding, extra baggage, or in-flight meals, these fees will be clearly disclosed to you during the booking process. You have the choice to add or decline these services based on your preferences.

No Surprise Charges

Rest assured that when you complete your booking with TravelQuads, there won't be any unexpected charges or fees added to your ticket. The price you agree to during the booking process is what you'll pay.

Price Guarantee

We stand by our commitment to transparent pricing. Once you've booked your ticket, the price is locked in, and you won't be subject to any additional fees, even if there are changes or updates to the fare rules or airline policies.

TravelQuads believes in providing a hassle-free and fair booking experience for all our customers. If you have any questions or concerns about our pricing or fees, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team for assistance.
