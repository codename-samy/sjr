PRICE MATCH PROMISE

Experience the Airline Fare Center Advantage: Our Price Match Promise

When you choose Airline Fare Center for your bookings, you're guaranteed the best available prices.

Our commitment to providing the most competitive fares is backed by our Price Match Promise (PMP). With PMP, we ensure that you get the lowest prices for your travel needs. Here's how it works:

What Does 'Same Itinerary' Mean?

'Same itinerary' means every detail of your booking matches precisely. This includes the airline, flight number, travel date, the number of passengers, and seat type.

Why Should PMP Bookings Be 'Offered and Available'?

For a successful PMP claim, the lower-priced itinerary you discover must be live and available for booking on a competitor's website when you contact us. Our customer care representatives will verify this information.

How to Invoke PMP?

If you wish to invoke our Price Match Promise, please reach out to us at info@airlinefarecenter.com or call us at 1-631-309-0576. Provide your Airline Fare Center booking reference number and all the details of the lower-priced itinerary, including the airline, flight, itinerary, date, number of passengers, and seat type. Additionally, share the URL or screenshot to facilitate our customer service representatives' verification process. Please note that incomplete or unverified claims may be denied. After verification, you'll have the option to keep your existing itinerary or receive a refund of the price difference.

Are There Any Exceptions to PMP?

Exclusions: Our Price Match Promise does not apply to military, student, government, tour operator, bulk, or vacation package fares. Additionally, fare errors are also excluded from this guarantee.
